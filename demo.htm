About Entech

Entech

EnTech Consulting Corporation (EnTech) provides air quality and remediation services to private businesses, industry and government agencies across the country. With roots in environmental compliance and natural resource management, EnTech works on behalf of its clients to avoid or reduce project impacts to the environment while keeping projects moving forward.

Management Team

Meet the Entech Management Team

In 2013, Entech became a 100% employee-owned company by adopting an Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP). Providence is managed under the direction of  Karen Holden, CEO, and supported by several company executives and principals that comprise the Providence Board of Directors. The board includes Karen Holden, Todd Black, Yousheng Zeng, Rich Major, and Clay Breaud with Jason Benoit and Mike Purdom serving as advisors.

Jeff Lu

President and Senior Air Consultant

Dr. Lu has twenty-five years of experience consulting, managing, and providing environmental engineering services within the oil and gas industry. His experience includes air permitting, compliance, and advanced modeling, interpretation, and development of guidance for PSD, NNSR, NSR, Title V Operating permits, NSPS, NESHAP, and MACT under Federal Clean Air Act requirements. Dr. Lu also has extensive experience with GHG protocol including baseline development, monitoring and measuring, RQ/MSS database development, flare/combustor efficiency evaluation, and environmental air compliance auditing. He has negotiated several NOVs and Consent orders, as well as having experience in emission credit generation and trading, and air pollution control process design and permitting. Dr. Lu has developed several successful air regulatory and compliance programs for major corporations and brings with him the knowledge and experience to apply these skills on the local, state, and federal level.
